AMT Digital Toolbox

An app designed for today's aircaft maintenance technicians

An all-in-one app for aviation mechanics

Use your phone for flight control rigging

Use your mobile device's built-in clinometer for rigging flight controls, verifying flight control travel, measuring prop rotation during engine timing.

Contains several useful calculators

All the calculators you need in one place: a torque wrench extension calculator, a weight and balance calculator, a rudder travel calculator (convert travel from degrees to inches), and more.

Access FAA regulations and publications

Use the app to access FAA regulations, Airworthiness Directives, Advisory Circulars, and other publications.

Future plans for a professional kit

A professional kit that will contain bluetooth enabled clinometers, cable tensiometer, and mag timing light that will automatically record and save measurements to your device or computer.
